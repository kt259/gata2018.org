ABSTRACT GUIDELINES

Abstracts should be submitted only online. Extended deadline: 20 April 2018.

  • - The abstract (250 words max.) must be typed in plain letters, single-spaced using Arial 10-pitch type letters (justified).
  • - Abstract title should be typed in lowercase bold letters.
  • - Include name(s) of author(s) in a new line in bold letters, putting initials after the surname (for example Somebody S.).
  • - The name of the Presenting Author should be underlined (for example Author P.)
  • - The name of Institution(s) must be in a separate new line in italic letters.
  • - Presenting Author must register for the Conference before the abstract is accepted.
  • - Short talks will be selected by the organisers from the abstracts.

For any further information please contact effie@meetis.gr.
